// Qualification Frameworks

The right methodology
for your motion.

We diagnose your sales motion and apply the methodology that fits, then embed it into your team's muscle memory.

Best for: Full lifecycle
Bowtie-native
SPICED
Situation, Pain, Impact, Critical Event, Decision. Designed for the modern revenue cycle, maps directly to the bowtie and extends naturally into retention and expansion motions.
SaaSPLGCS-led growthExpansion revenue
Best for: Enterprise
Gold standard
MEDDPICC
Metrics, Economic Buyer, Decision Criteria, Decision Process, Paper Process, Identify Pain, Champion, Competition. The most rigorous framework for complex multi-stakeholder enterprise deals.
EnterpriseLong cyclesMulti-stakeholderHigh ACV
Best for: SMB / Mid-market
Discovery-led
CHAMP
Challenges, Authority, Money, Prioritization. Starts with the buyer's challenges rather than budget, ideal for consultative selling where understanding pain drives urgency.
SMBMid-marketConsultativeFast cycles
Best for: High-velocity
Classic
BANT
Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline. Simple and effective for high-velocity transactional sales. Best used as a light qualification filter rather than a full discovery framework.
